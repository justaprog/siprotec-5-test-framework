# Electrical Terms in Plain Language
This glossary provides simple explanations of key electrical engineering terms relevant to the SIPROTEC 5 test framework.

- Phase: one of the three electrical lines, A/B/C
- Current: amount of electrical flow, measured here as `Ia`, `Ib`, `Ic` for each phase
- Voltage: electrical potential, often written `Va`, `Vb`, `Vc`
- Pickup Current: Threshold above which protection should react
- Fault Current: abnormal high current during a fault
- Sample: one measurement at one point in time
- Sampling rate: how often samples are generated per second
